Sports Specific Injury Prevention

Overview

Problem and solution

Facts about sports injuries

- There is an estimated 3.5 million or more sports related injuries per year in the U.S.

- There is no emphasis on educating athletes on injury prevention

- Only so much can be done after an injury is sustained

Problem

Athletes, whether they are professionals, college students, or just everyday individuals, frequently encounter injuries. These can occur during training or competition. When an athlete is injured, these injuries demand considerable recovery time and can oftentimes leave athletes unable to perform at the same level they could before being injured. Common orthopedic medicine focuses primarily on post-injury recovery, and fewer and fewer resources are being devoted to preventing injury for those athletes.
